Usage: meza legacyimport <environment name>

No directives. Overwrite the entire environment with the contents of
a legacy installation. Use of this command requires that the
following be present in your config, probably in:

/opt/meza/config/local-secret/<environment name>/group_vars/all.yml

legacy_remote:
  host: example.com
  db_user: root
  db_pass: password1234

(fill in appropriate values)
